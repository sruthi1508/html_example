Active player in the maritime software space

MarinoTech Services have been an active player in the maritime software space over the past 25 years with the rich legacy of delivering cutting edge ship management solutions.  Under the brand name Shipmate, we have successfully catered to the needs of ship manager, manning agents and Ship owners worldwide by offering solutions related to Crewing, Payroll, Procurement, Planned Maintenance, Vessel performance monitoring, HSQE,Seafarer Engagement Portal and Work Hours- Rest Hours of seafarer. Our crew competency management software based on DNV standards helps with systematic training and development officers to take up higher responsibilities. Through Marinotech services(MTS), we plan to further enhance our relentless commitment to the maritime sector by offering advanced SaaS software that will help navigate specific complex challenges which could help enhance operational efficiency, productivity and profitability. These solutions focuses on  aspects relating to recruitment, certificate checking, training & competence analysis, PDA and similar areas for which currently available automation is not robust. The SaaS software under the MTS umbrella are GDPR Certified, SOC2 Type I Attested  and ISO 27001 Certified thus guaranteeing conformance with all international legislations and its adherence to security standards and industry regulatory requirements.
